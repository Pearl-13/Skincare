SIMPLE HACKS FOR A NATURALLY GLOWING SKIN.


Looking for a simple skincare routine for a glowing skin? You are not alone. Here is a short guide showing natural, affordable and simple hacks you can start right now.

Hydration: Water they say is life. Drinking sufficient quantity of water- 2-3 litres for adult, daily cleanses the body, keeps the skin plump and maintains elasticity. Dehydrated skin looks dull and ages faster, so make wayer your most essential skin care product

Fruits and Vegetables: Load up on fruits like oranges, avocados, berries, omega-3 rich foods like fish and nuts. Avoiding diary, processed sugar and fatty foods if your skin is oily and prone to breakout.

Exfoliation: Gentle, regular removal of dead skin using honey and sugar one to two times a week to reveal glowing skin.

Sleep: The power of getting enough sleep cannot be underestimated as it helps prevent inflammation, dullness and eyebags.

Natural Oils and Butter: Oils and butters like coconut oil, olive oil, cocoa butter and shea butter moisturizes, heal and protect the skin. For best results, apply them when the skin is damp.

Here are key mistakes to avoid to get that glow

  • Avoid harsh soaps and bleaching products.

  • Exposure to sunlight.

  • Smoking, Poor diet, dehydration and stress.

  • Constant picking of the face.

  • Use of dirty pillowcases or makeup brushes.
